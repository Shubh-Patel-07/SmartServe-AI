# 🍽️ SmartServe AI

> AI Powered QR Based Restaurant Ordering System

SmartServe AI is a modern restaurant ordering platform that allows customers to scan a QR code, browse the digital menu, place food orders, and receive AI-powered assistance without waiting for a waiter.

This project was developed as part of the **GTU Skill Based Training Program 2026** in collaboration with **BharatCares (CSRBOX)** and **IBM SkillsBuild**.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📱 QR Code Based Ordering
- 🍔 Digital Restaurant Menu
- 🛒 Smart Cart System
- 🤖 AI Powered Chatbot
- 💬 Food Recommendations
- 📋 Table Number Ordering
- 📱 Mobile Responsive Design
- ⚡ Fast and Contactless Ordering

## 📌 Project Workflow

1. Customer scans the QR code.
2. Digital menu opens instantly.
3. Customer browses food items.
4. Items are added to the cart.
5. Customer places the order.
6. AI chatbot answers menu related questions.
7. Restaurant receives the order.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The objective of SmartServe AI is to modernize restaurant ordering by replacing manual ordering with a simple, fast, and AI-powered digital solution.
